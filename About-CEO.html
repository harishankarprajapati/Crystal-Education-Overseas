About CEO

Mr. Ashish Sinha, the esteemed Director of Crystal Education Overseas, proudly shares the remarkable journey and expertise of his company. Mr. Sinha is an MBA graduate from Indian Institute of Management (IIM) Kozhikode with majors in Strategy, Marketing & HR. He secured 99.52 percentile in Common Admission Test (CAT) conducted by the IIMs. He is a luminary in the field of business & management and covered by reputed media houses regularly.

With a legacy of training thousands of students, Crystal Education Overseas stands as a beacon of excellence in the realm of overseas education services. Mr. Sinha has personally mentored more than 40,000 students. He asserts “What makes a brand is the people behind it. We have the best team to guide you in your professional life journey”.
